About Us

We specialize in customised interior design to meet the needs and views of our customers.
Our last 10 years of successful operation with zero complaints is a milestone we strive to keep up in future.

Our History

Started in 2010, Design Fusion quickly established itself as a interior designer icon par excellence in New Delhi. With its unique brand of world fusion decor that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele in entire India. Featuring some of the best designing ideas in the world, you never know what will arrive on your wall the next time you visit us.

The company traces its humble beginnings to The Room changer, a successful chain started by our CEO, Mr. Vaibhav Dubey, that featured for the first time the world's best interior decorators.

Corporate Leadership

Vaibhav Dubey Chief Executive Officer

Our CEO, Vaibhav, credits his hardworking parents who always supported him in his journey to become an interior designer and letting him pursue his passion with the intention of giving their children the best future. His mother's creativity to decorate their house with whatever unique piece of furnishing they could get from annual fares which have items from all over india was his inspiration to create fusion interiors for which The Room Changer became well known. He brings his zeal for fusion interiors to this company, pioneering cross-cultural interior connections.

Ananya Singh Chief Design Officer

Our CDO, Ananya, the star designer and valedictorian of her batch, comes from a long established family tradition in arts and designing. Her experiences growing up in an palace privately owned by her family in the South Indian outback gave her great appreciation for varieties of interior design sources. As she puts it in her own words, Everything that looks good, wins, and everything that feels good, pays!

Om Vashishtha Chief Color Officer

Blessed with the most discerning vibrant visual sense, Om, our CCO, personally ensures that every item that we design meets his exacting tastes and perfectly fits the customers demands. Our chefs dread the tongue lashing that ensues if their designes does not meet his exacting standards. He lives by his motto, You click only if you survive my wits.

Harshit Agarwal Chief Room Designer

Award winning three-star New York designer with wide International experience having worked closely with whos-who in the architecture world, he specializes in creating mind-boggling Indo-American fusion designes. He says, Put together the ideas from the two craziest cultures, and you get a winning hit! Voila!
